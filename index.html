How to Play

Controls

  • Arrow Up / Down: Change thrust or speed
  • Arrow Left / Right: Tilt or pitch the plane
  • Spacebar: Fire machine guns
  • X: Launch a missile
  • C: Eject from your plane
  • Mouse click or tap (mobile): Flap or rise, then release to fall

Tips & Tricks for Bit planes

  • Practice throttle control, small changes keep you alive longer.
  • Use altitude to dodge and to aim, higher ground helps in fights.
  • Fire missiles when close, they have limited range but big impact.
  • Eject if your plane is about to explode, respawn and return faster.
  • Watch how other pilots move, copy smart moves and avoid bad ones.
  • Try different modes, some teach survival while others teach aiming.
  • Keep calm in crowded matches, a steady hand beats panic flying.
